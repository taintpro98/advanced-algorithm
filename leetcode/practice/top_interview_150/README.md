# Top Interview 150

This folder contains solutions and notes for LeetCode's Top Interview 150 problem set.

The Top Interview 150 is a curated collection of the most frequently asked coding interview questions across major tech companies. These problems cover a wide range of topics including arrays, strings, linked lists, trees, graphs, dynamic programming, and more.

Use this space to track your progress through the playlist and maintain personal notes on each problem's approach, edge cases, and optimizations.
